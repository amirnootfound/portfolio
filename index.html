My Story

Inspirational Moment of this year

2024: A Year of Growth, Dreams, and Determination 2024 has been a transformative and inspiring year in my life. It is the year I embraced my dreams, overcame challenges, and set my sights on a bright future in programming and studying abroad.

Advancing My Passion for Programming Programming has always been more than a skill for me—it’s a way to create, innovate, and make a positive impact on the world. In 2024, I deepened my knowledge through practice and exploration. I worked with tools like Ngrok, learning its applications in securing connections, and began exploring PHP and its role in social engineering. I challenged myself to think like a problem solver and continuously push my boundaries. I found inspiration in shows like *“Mr. Robot”* and *“Cyberstalker.”* These shows not only entertained me but also motivated me to explore hacking techniques, vulnerabilities, and the power of cybersecurity. My goal has been to surpass fictional characters like Luke, proving that with knowledge and determination, I can achieve even greater things. I also revisited my frontend development skills, recalling the courses I took in the past. My desire to create impactful programs, VPNs, applications for computers, and chip-related software became the driving force behind my efforts. I envisioned a future where my technological innovations could improve people’s lives.

Preparing for Studying Abroad 2024 has been the year when I solidified my dream of studying abroad. After years of planning, I took significant steps to make this dream a reality. I began actively preparing for English proficiency tests, marking a turning point in my academic journey. I worked hard to gather necessary documents, obtain recommendation letters, and enhance my knowledge. This process was not just about preparation; it was about proving to myself that I could overcome any obstacle. I found inspiration in my brother, who is currently studying abroad. His journey has shown me that with dedication and hard work, one can succeed in a new country, begin their career, and achieve their educational goals. His story fueled my ambition and gave me hope.

Financial Independence and Family Support Another significant aspect of 2024 has been my pursuit of financial independence. I delved into trading and cryptocurrency to contribute to funding my education. Setting a goal to save a substantial amount of money, I worked to balance my studies, personal projects, and financial ambitions. Throughout this journey, my family played a crucial role. My parents’ belief in my potential motivated me to aim higher. Their support reminded me that I am not alone on this path.

Dreaming Big and Staying Inspired What truly made 2024 special was the clarity of my vision for the future. I imagined myself studying IT abroad, developing my skills, and ultimately working in the tech field to make a meaningful contribution to society. This dream is about more than personal success; it’s about creating a better future through technology. The dream of freedom and endless possibilities inspired me. I aspired to travel the world, learn about new cultures, and build meaningful connections. This vision gave me the strength to stay focused and motivated, even during challenging times.

Conclusion 2024 has been a year of discovery, growth, and resilience. It has taught me the value of hard work, the importance of dreaming big, and the power of believing in myself. I am ready to turn my aspirations into reality and continue my journey toward a life filled with purpose, innovation, and success.
